# Learning Objectives - Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

1. **Define Physical AI** - Articulate the fundamental concept of Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional digital AI systems
2. **Explain Embodied Intelligence** - Describe how intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its environment
3. **Compare Digital vs Physical AI** - Identify key differences between AI systems operating in digital spaces versus those interacting with the physical world
4. **Recognize Sensor-Actuator Roles** - Understand the importance of sensors and actuators in enabling AI systems to perceive and manipulate the physical environment
5. **Appreciate Real-time Constraints** - Explain why real-time processing is critical for AI systems operating in physical environments
6. **Identify Applications** - Recognize practical applications where Physical AI excels compared to traditional AI approaches

These objectives will guide your learning journey through the fundamentals of Physical AI, establishing a foundation for more advanced concepts in subsequent chapters.
